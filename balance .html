FEATURED AUTHORS

Stephen King: A Horror Legend Stephen King is a master of horror and suspense, who has written over 60 novels and 200 short stories, many of which have been adapted into films and TV shows.

Joanne Rowling CH OBE FRSL, better known by her pen name J. K. Rowling, is a British author and philanthropist. She wrote Harry Potter, a seven-volume fantasy series published from 1997 to 2007

Margaret Atwood is a Canadian writer best known for her prose fiction and for her feminist perspective. Role reversal and new beginnings are recurrent themes in her novels, all of them centred on women seeking their relationship

Haruki Murakami was born in Kyoto, Japan, on January 12th, 1949. He graduated from Waseda University in Tokyo and ran a jazz bar with his wife for seven years before becoming an author. He is most known for his captivating and bestselling novels

John Green, American author who writes realistic fiction for young adults. Reviewers have praised Green's work for his bright yet troubled characters and thoughtful treatment of difficult topics.

Angela Thomas was born in 1988 in Jackson, Mississippi. She grew up in an economically depressed African American neighborhood and was exposed to gun violence at an early age. Thomas was interested in hip-hop as a form of expression .
